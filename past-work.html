Firecracker 100: Lernerville Speedway

The firecracker 100 is a a major event held yearly at Lernerville Speedeay in Saver PA. We were ask by the speedway to design and build a website just for this event. the site helps fans buy tickets for the event also has all the information any fan would need to know.

RJ's Brewery and Grill

RJ's Brewery is a local resturant in our area that asked us to design and build their website. The site has their general information any customer may be looking for. the site also has a full listing of their craft beers they make in-house, and it allows customers to place orders that they can then go and pick up at the brewery. We also built out the full kitcken menu the RJ's offers which customers can use to place orders for pickup or delivery.
